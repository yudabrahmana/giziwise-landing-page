Jelajahi Dunia Nutrisi dengan GiziWise

We are C23-PS450 | Product-Based Bangkit Capstone Project Team

Abstract

The project aims to develop a mobile app for tracking the nutritional content of food. The app will use the latest nutritional data, consider regional variations, and focus on simplicity, accuracy, and ease of use. In the end, the app aims to empower users to make informed decisions, improve their health, and reduce healthcare costs.

Tools/IDE/Library

The app will use the latest nutritional data, consider regional variations, and focus on simplicity, accuracy, and ease of use. Design thinking will be used to ensure it is user-centered.

Machine Learning

Our machine learning will involve a Convolutional Neural Network algorithm that uses the TensorFlow library.

Cloud Computing

Our back end tech stack will be Nodejs, Typescript, Fastify, Sequelize, and Postgres.

Mobile Development

Our Mobile Development capstone project involves creating a Kotlin-based Android app using Android Studio as the IDE.

Articles

Many people lack an understanding of the nutritional content of their food, making it difficult to maintain a healthy diet. The app can address these issues by providing users with a simple tool to track their food intake and make informed decisions. Additionally, the availability of packaged and processed foods has made it challenging to know what one is consuming and its impact on health.
